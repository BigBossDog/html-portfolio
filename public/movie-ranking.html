Highly acclaimed titles 2023

Monster Hunter Rise

Monster Hunter Rise is an action role-playing video game developed and published by Capcom for the Nintendo Switch.

One Piece Odessey

One Piece Odyssey is a turn-based role-playing game in which players control Monkey D. Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates

Sea Of Stars

Sea of Stars is a role-playing game (RPG) centered around two heroes named Valere and Zale, who use the power of the sun and moon to fight against an alchemist.
